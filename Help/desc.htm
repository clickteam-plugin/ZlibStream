Description

The ZlibStream object is a single-file compression utility, enabling you to compress and decompress single files under Zlib format.
This extension is multi-threaded and threadsafe.
That means when you compress or decompress, your MMF2 application will not pause until the process is done. You can run as many compressions (and decompressions) as you want simultaneously, and your application will not slow down.
You can access more information on the official ZlibStream site or read through the ZlibStream topic on Clickteam forums.

Legend:
When referencing to other parts of the extension:
Blue: Expression.
Yellow: Parameter.
Cyan: Condition.
Red: Action.
Purple: Property.

Notes:
1. Running several compressions and/or decompressions will not slow down your application.
2. If the buffer is larger than the file, the buffer is lowered to the size of the file.
3. The buffer size is limited from 1 to 16384. By default it is 8192.
